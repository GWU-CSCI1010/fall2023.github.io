CSCI 1010 Computer Science Orientation

Welcome to CSCI 1010, Computer Science Orientation! This course serves as your gateway to the exciting world of computer science. Whether you’re a novice or have some prior experience, this course will provide you with a strong foundation in key concepts and practices.

Course Overview

Instructor: Professor John Doe Term: Fall 2023 Location: Room 123, Main Building

Course Description

CSCI 1010 is designed to introduce you to the fundamental principles of computer science. Topics covered include:

  • Understanding the basics of algorithms and data structures.
  • Learning to program in Python.
  • Exploring the role of computation in various fields.
  • Gaining problem-solving skills through hands-on projects.
  • Preparing for more advanced computer science courses.

What to Expect

  • Engaging lectures and interactive discussions.
  • Practical coding exercises and projects.
  • Access to online resources and tutorials.
  • Collaborative learning with fellow students.
  • Guidance from experienced teaching staff.

Resources

To excel in this course, you will need access to:

  • A personal computer with internet connectivity.
  • Python development environment (Anaconda recommended).
  • Required textbooks and course materials (see syllabus).
